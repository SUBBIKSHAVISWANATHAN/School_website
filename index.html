About Us

DVM School is committed to providing quality education... Dalmia Vidya Mandir, Dalmiapuram is a CBSE English Medium Senior Secondary Co-Educational School located at Dalmiapuram, Trichy District, Tamil Nadu. It owes its inception to the profound vision of the Dalmia family. The school upholds the founder's commitment to excellence in all fields, with emphasis on its motto “NYAYA AUR SWADHARM SE RASHTRA”. We are pioneers in quality education with excellent board results and a list of Awards and Medals at the State and National levels. The Management of Dalmia Vidya Mandir has rich experience in running educational institutions at different locations of the country pan India. We endeavor to harmonize the aims of our school with the principles of universal brotherhood, the aspiration of our students, and the need of our nation. Our school ensures academic excellence, intellectual growth, art, dance, music, sports, a high standard of ethical awareness, and community service.

Dr. Rosetta Williams - Principal
